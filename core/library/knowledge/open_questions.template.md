# Open Questions

*Questions raised during the lab's lifetime that haven't yet been resolved.
The director surfaces these in every brief; the consolidator promotes
resolved questions out (with their answer) and demotes stale ones.*

## Conventions

- One H2 per question (e.g., `## Q-001 — Does Mamba scale beyond 7B?`)
- Body: context, what would resolve it, current best guess, why it matters
- When resolved, add `## Status: RESOLVED at C{N}` subsection + answer
- When killed (out-of-scope/never-answerable), mark `## Status: KILLED` with reason
